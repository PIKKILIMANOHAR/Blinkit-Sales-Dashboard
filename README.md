# Blinkit-Sales-Dashboard (Interactive Dashboard Creation Using Power BI)

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze Blinkit's sales performance using Power BI. This dashboard provides insights into sales trends, product performance, outlet characteristics, and customer ratings. It enables businesses to understand sales distribution across different outlet types, sizes, and locations, helping improve inventory management, marketing strategies, and overall business decision-making.

## Dataset Used
- <a href="https://github.com/PIKKILIMANOHAR/Blinkit-Sales-Dashboard/blob/main/blinkit_sales_data.csv">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)
- What are the total sales generated?
- What is the average sales per transaction?
- How many items have been sold?
- What is the average customer rating?
- How do sales vary by outlet establishment year?
- Which item type contributes the highest sales?
- How do Low Fat and Regular products compare in sales?
- Which outlet size generates the highest revenue?
- Which outlet location (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3) performs the best?
- Which outlet type has the highest sales and number of items?
- What is the average item visibility across outlet types?

- Dashboard Interaction <a href="https://github.com/PIKKILIMANOHAR/Blinkit-Sales-Dashboard/blob/main/blinkit_dashboard.png">View Dashboard</a>

## Process
- Collected and imported the Blinkit sales dataset into Power BI.
- Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query.
- Created DAX measures to calculate Total Sales, Average Sales, Number of Items, and Average Rating.
- Designed interactive visualizations including cards, charts, slicers, and tables.
- Added filters for Outlet Location Type, Outlet Size, and Item Type to enable dynamic analysis.
- Generated meaningful business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

## Project Insights
- Total sales reached *$1.20M* across all outlets.
- The average sales per transaction is *$141*.
- Approximately *9,000 items* were sold.
- The average customer rating is *3.92*, indicating positive customer satisfaction.
- Regular Fat products generated higher sales than Low Fat products.
- Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the highest-selling product categories.
- Medium-sized outlets contributed the highest share of total sales.
- Tier 3 outlet locations generated the highest revenue.
- Supermarket Type 1 recorded the highest sales and item count among all outlet types.
- Outlets established around *2018* achieved the highest sales performance.

## Final Conclusion
The Blinkit Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance across products and outlets. The findings indicate that Medium-sized outlets, Tier 3 locations, and Supermarket Type 1 contribute significantly to overall revenue. Additionally, product categories such as Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods drive the majority of sales. This dashboard helps businesses make informed decisions regarding inventory planning, outlet performance, and sales optimization, ultimately supporting business growth and profitability.
